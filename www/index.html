What is this thing?

  • Free shell hosting!
  • A fun community!
  • Early Internet nostalgia!
  • A silly social experiment
  • A system engineer wasting time

Welcome to tilde.city, Yet Another Clone of the tilde.club idea! tilde.city is being started with the goal of high reliability and availability, as well as maximum transparency and setup repeatability, while still remaining a free and fun community. How do we plan to do that? Thanks to the fine folks at Cloud At Cost our VPSes are really cheap, and non-recurring, and you can help too by sending us a few Satoshis of Bitcoin to 1ti1deiBDTTPpKYq6rRrEGb3Y8UY45AGf or using the donation link below to get a unique address (but it'll all wind up in the same place).

Interested in tilde.city/~yourtilde? Create yourself an SSH public key (GitHub has a great article on how!) and e-mail it along with your desired username to twilde@tilde.city! While we're getting started, check out tilde.club's FAQ for more ideas on how to get rolling.

We now have our own FAQ too!
